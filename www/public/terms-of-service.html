7 Shared Wallets & Bill Splitting App

Money Tracker includes collaborative features that allow you to share budget wallets with family members or friends, and to split bills between multiple people.

Shared Wallets

  • As the wallet owner, you can invite other registered users by email to join a shared wallet.
  • All members of a shared wallet can view transactions, categories, and wallet goals.
  • You are responsible for only inviting people who have consented to participate and to have their data shared within that wallet.
  • By inviting someone, you confirm you have their permission to submit their name and email address to us for the purpose of sending a single invitation email.

Bill Splitting

  • The bill-splitting feature calculates how to divide an expense between named participants.
  • Split calculations are for convenience only. Money Tracker does not process any actual payments or money transfers between users.
  • Any real-world financial settlements between split participants are entirely between those individuals. We have no role in, and no liability for, any resulting financial transactions.

Friend Management

  • You may add other users as friends using their unique user code (MT-XXXXXX) to facilitate shared budgeting and split tracking.
  • Friend requests must be accepted by the recipient before a friendship connection is established.
  • You must not use the friend or invite features to harass, spam, or solicit other users.
